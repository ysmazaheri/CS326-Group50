Mission Statement

UMass Study Bites aims to revolutionize the dining experience for UMass Amherst students by providing a convenient and efficient food delivery service directly from on-campus dining halls. Our app is designed to help students access delicious meals without the need to leave their dorms, especially during times of illness or injury when going to a dining hall may be challenging. By offering a user-friendly interface that integrates live UMass menus, Study Bites streamlines the ordering process, saving students valuable time, particularly during busy periods like midterms and finals. Our commitment is to provide a safe, reliable, and innovative solution that enhances the overall campus living experience.
